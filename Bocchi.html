Bocchi the Rock!

Hitori "Bokki" Goto is an introverted girl. In middle school, she started playing guitar, wanting to join a band. But since she had no friends, she ended up practicing guitar for six hours every day by herself. After becoming an accomplished guitarist, she made a video of herself playing guitar under the name "Guitar Hero" and fantasized about spreading across the school's cultural spectrum. But not only could she not find bandmates, before she realized it, she was in high school and still couldn't make a single friend! One day, Nijika Ijichi, the drummer of Kessoku Band, contacted her. And because of that, her daily life began to change little by little.
